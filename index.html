About

Professional Sound, Lighting, DJ & Karaoke Services

Based in Sydney Mines and North Sydney, TC Audio Productions has been serving clients since 1992 with professional sound and lighting solutions, open format disc jockey services, and karaoke for a wide range of events. From Centre 200, the Keltic Lodge, and Inverary Inn to community halls, private venues, weddings, proms, fundraisers, and community functions, the focus is always on dependable service, strong presentation, and music that fits the room.
